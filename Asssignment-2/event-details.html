Skill Bloom - Event Details

GreenHands Skill Fair

On April 24th, 2025 – At Green Park.

Join us for a day of earth-friendly skills and community bonding! GreenHands focuses on gardening, composting, sustainable crafts, and DIY home solutions. Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just curious about eco-living, this fair offers hands-on workshops and local experts ready to share their knowledge.

Create & Connect: Art and Craft Fair

On April 26th, 2025 – At Architect Auditorium.

Unleash your creativity at the Create & Connect Fair—a colorful celebration of art, design, and handmade wonders. From painting and origami to calligraphy and clay modeling, discover new techniques, meet local artists, and even showcase your own talents. No experience needed—just imagination!
